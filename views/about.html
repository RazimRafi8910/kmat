About KMAT

KMAT KERALA is the entrance examination for candidates seeking admission to various MBA courses in Kerala. It is a State level exam which is usually held twice a year, the upcoming exam is yet to be announced.

Information to Candidates for Upcoming KMAT KERALA EXAM 2023

ELIGIBILITY To apply for KMAT 2023, candidates should possess a Bachelor’s degree in any stream recognized from Kerala state or any other recognized university. Final year students of Graduate Courses can also apply.
